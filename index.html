My Portfolio of Communication Projects

Ancentral Knowledge and the Pharmaceutical Industry

“Raíces que curan” is a campaign that seeks to raise awareness about the appropriation of ancestral knowledge by pharmaceutical companies. This campaign seeks to promote a bioethical relationship between the pharmaceutical industry and the Amazonian indigenous people of Ecuador through the education of 38 students of Colegio Americano de Quito.

Learn More

Internal Communication Audit at J.W. Marriot Quito

A communication audit was performed to understand the communication status of the organization. The analysis was done through observations and surveys answered by employees of J.W. Marriott Quito. As a response to the communication audit, the campaign "Open the doors to a world of opportunities" was suggested to give new opportunities for the personal of professional growth, recognition, and a new communication channel to create a bidirectional communication with higher positions.

Learn More

Comedy Night

"Night of Laughter" was a stand-up comedy event with comedian Christoph Baumann to raise funds for the Nahuel Foundation through sponsorship and ticket sales. A profit of 52% was obtained that was donated to the non-profit organization to continue with its projects.

Campaña Desconéctalos Awareness Campaign

A social marketing campaign to bring awareness to the responsible use of technology for young kids. Aimed at parents, the campaign was held in a public park where recreational games were held, for both children and parents, to disconnect them from technology.

Helen Doron Communication Audit

An audit that solved communication problems found through surveys, personal interviews and diagnosis of communication tools with the purpose of improving the working environment of Helen Doron Matrix Ecuador. The implementation of the "Learning and playing with Kevin" campaign was suggested and chosen by the company. It eventually won the internal communication campaign winner competition.

PJ's Startup

PJ's was an entrepreneurship idea that offered customized unisex pajama pants. The project began with a lack of youthful and customized pajama pants designs for university students. Permission was obtained from Cartoon Network for the use of its animations. Over 150 pants were sold in just one week, allowing for a return on investment.
