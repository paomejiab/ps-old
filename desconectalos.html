About the campaign

Desconéctalos

Background: The campaign "Desconéctalos" attempts to bring awareness to a latent problem in society: parents that promote the unhealthy use of technology towards their children at a very early age.

This social marketing campaign had the objective of creating awareness for parents about the responsible use of technology for kids. The campaign was held in a public park where recreational games were held for both children and parents and disconnect them from technological devices, all while educating them about healthy uses of technology. Our goal was to help parents avoid situations where they give devices like phones to children to keep them calm.

Through our social marketing campaign, we got 32 parents during our exercise to commit to disconnecting their children from technology. We additionally got them to sign our wooden board, increasing our message to the public.
