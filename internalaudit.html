About the audit

J.W. Marriott Quito

Background: J.W. Marriott Quito is the Quito base of the international hotel chain J.W. Marriott. The hotel is reknowned throughout the city for its luxury experience and modern accomodations.

A communication audit was performed to understand the communication status within the organization. The analysis was done through observations and surveys answered by employees of J.W. Marriott Quito. As a response to the communication audit, the campaign "Open the doors to a world of opportunities" was suggested to give new opportunities for hotel personnel, relating to professional growth, recognition, and the creation of a new communication channel to create a bidirectional communication with upper management.

The campaign was presented to the Human Resources Manager, Country Manager, and Director of Marketing at J.W. Marriott Quito. The campaign was taken as consideration for future applications and was sent to the international J.W. Marriott marketing team for further use.

See images See audit report

A map was created to understand the divisions of the departments. This allowed us to understand the flow of information within the organization. As observed in the map, the areas of the company do not have many subdivisions which promotes the transmission of information, and the good use of the different communication tools that are available within the hotel. Similarly, having an organizational pyramid with few levels allows associates to establish friendly and professional relationships, which improves the wellbeing of the company.

A pre-diagnosis of the hotel's communication channels was made. The analysis of the information of the billboards located in each department of the company was carried out. It was identified that Marriott shares its institutional values in all departments. The information provided by the billboards is aligned to each respective department, based on established criteria. This pre-diagnosis analyzed 58 communication tools in use within the organization.

We conducted additional analysis on company brand images, like the Marriott logo. Through our analysis, we identified the specific version of the logo that resonated best with the employees at the hotel. We communicated our findings to management, in order to help them unify small mediums of communication like the logo.
